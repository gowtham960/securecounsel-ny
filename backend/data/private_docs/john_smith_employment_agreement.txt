Title: John Smith Employment Agreement

Section 1. Employment Role.
John Smith is employed as a senior sales manager for Acme Medical Devices LLC.

Section 7. Non-Competition.
Employee shall not engage in a competing business within New York State for a period of two years following termination of employment.

Section 8. Confidential Information.
Employee shall not disclose client lists, pricing strategies, sales methods, trade secrets, or confidential business information.

Section 9. Non-Solicitation.
For twelve months following termination, Employee shall not solicit clients with whom Employee had material contact during the last year of employment.

Section 10. Termination.
Either party may terminate employment with thirty days written notice. The employer may terminate immediately for cause.
